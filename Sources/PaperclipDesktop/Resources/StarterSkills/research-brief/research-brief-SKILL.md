# SKILL.md

## Purpose

Help an agent collect evidence, compare sources, and write brief research summaries that are easy to act on.

## When to Use

- the task requires research or comparison
- the answer needs supporting evidence
- the user needs a recommendation, not just raw notes

## Workflow

1. Clarify the research question.
2. Gather the most relevant evidence first.
3. Compare sources for agreement and disagreement.
4. Summarize what matters for the decision.
5. End with a recommendation and any open questions.

## Guardrails

- keep research proportional to the task
- separate facts from inference
- highlight missing evidence
- avoid pretending uncertainty does not exist

## Output Style

- summary first
- evidence second
- recommendation last
